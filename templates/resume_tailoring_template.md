## Step 2: Resume Positioning

One-line profile:

`Business/economics and supply-chain-oriented candidate with cross-cultural experience across [industry/company types], strong analytical execution, and English-Chinese communication strengths.`

Adjust based on the JD:

- Operations / Program Coordinator: execution, coordination, stakeholder management, process improvement.
- Supply Chain / S&OP: demand planning, inventory, data analysis, cross-functional planning.
- Customer Success / Implementation: client onboarding, problem solving, communication, adoption.
- Category / Buying / Merchandising: market research, assortment, consumer insight, brand/business strategy.

## Step 3: Bullet Formula

Use:

`Action verb + scope/context + measurable result + JD keyword`

Examples:

- Coordinated cross-functional tasks across [teams/markets], improving [process/outcome] by [number] while supporting [JD responsibility].
- Analyzed [dataset/process/customer behavior] to identify [insight], helping the team improve [metric] by [number].
- Supported [project/client/business area] by translating ambiguous needs into clear next steps, strengthening [stakeholder/customer/operational] outcomes.

## Step 5: Quality Check

- Job title in resume file name matches the JD.
- Summary changes for the role family.
- Top third of resume contains the strongest JD fit.
- Each main experience has at least one quantified bullet where truthful.
- No overclaiming German if the JD says German is only an advantage.
- Visa situation is handled accurately if mentioned.
